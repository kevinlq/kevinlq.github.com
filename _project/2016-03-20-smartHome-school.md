[想要下载，请点击这里](https://github.com/kevinlq/SmartHome-Qt)

## 开发环境

- windows7 64b,ubuntu 14.04 32b
- Qt4.8.6
- IAR:8.0
- Altium Designer 13.0
- arm-linux-gcc 4.4.3
- STM32,CC2530,ARM9
